In this year, Charles I became the second Stuart King, after the death of his father James I
Soon after his coronation as king, Parliament prevents Charles I from collecting Tonnage and Poundage for more than one year
Charles I needed money before he could fight with France and Spain, so he used a Forced Loan to raise money from the nobility
Five members of the nobility took the king to court over their imprisonment for refusing to pay the Forced Loan, but the king won
